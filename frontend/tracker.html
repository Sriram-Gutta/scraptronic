My Recycling

Log what you've recycled and watch your impact add up.

Coming in the next build.

A form to log a recycling entry (material, pounds, date, which center you went to), a running total of pounds and estimated $ value, points and tier progress (Seedling → Sapling → Tree → Forest), and a rough CO₂-saved estimate using EPA WARM model factors. All saved to your browser's localStorage — no signup, no tracking.

Your log stays in your browser. Nothing leaves your device.
